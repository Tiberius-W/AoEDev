A small pair of promos to let commander units specialize in beasts. No requirements on warbeast handling 1, so you can easily take some command limit ups or get battle command afterwards.

[ OVERVIEW ]
Warbeast Handling 1:
+ 25% vs cities (help reduce weakness of animals)
+ can receieve defensive bonuses (minor but nice buff)
+ no maintenence (can assign your spare animals to them between wars)

Warbeast handling 2:
+ commanded animals gain the strong promotion (+1 strength)
